WELCOME TO THE RLBS!

Welcome to the Rilesman League Bot Series! The RLBS is a twist on the popular league of legends esports scene, but with intermediate bots! The league consists of 8 teams and every team's captain and squad is centered around a friend of Rilesman's (see teams tab).
The RLBS will consist of a 4 week regular season with 2 days each week (Wednesday and Thursday). Then after 4 weeks, the top 6 teams will make it to a 2 week long double elimination playoffs.
All matches will be streamed on Rilesman's twitch and all games vods will be posted on Rilesman's youtube (see links tab). Hope you all tune in!
Games will start February 29th!
